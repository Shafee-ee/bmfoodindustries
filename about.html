About BM Organics

Delivering premium freeze-dried and dehydrated food products for global markets.

From a trusted foundation, a new organic vision is born.

Founded in 2017, BM Food Industries began with a simple vision, to make everyday cooking easier, healthier, and more natural. With the introduction of Kaaya, we brought traditional coconut preparation into a convenient, preservative-free form, trusted for its purity, taste, and simplicity.

Today, that journey evolves.

BM Organics is our step forward into ingredient-focused, naturally processed food solutions, bringing together innovation, sourcing, and modern food technology to meet today’s growing demand for clean, honest products.

Freeze-Dried Products

Dehydrated Ingredients

High-quality powders, slices, and flakes for versatile applications.

Export Quality

Products designed to meet global standards and requirements.
